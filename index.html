Ali Fahmi, PhD, MSc, BSc

I am a posdoctoral researcher in statistics and epidemiology in Professor Tjeerd van Staa's group at the School of Health Sciences in the University of Manchester. In the framework of the BRIT2 project, I do research on knowledge support for prescribing antibiotics to personalised their use. I also work on the effect of the Covid19 pandemic on the prescription of antibiotics and other consequent complications of using antibiotics.
