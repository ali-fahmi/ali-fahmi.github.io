Ali Fahmi, PhD, MSc, BSc

Am a postdoc at Uni of Manchester. Have received an NIHR DSE fellowship, focusing on adverse drug reactions in polypharmacy. Previously, conducted a ~3-year postdoc at Manchester and tried to make sense of health data, analysed the impact of COVID-19 pandemic, effectiveness of antibiotics on common infections, adverse drug reactions, drug-drug interactions, etc.

Love prediction models, explainability, knowledge graphs, and recently qualitative research like focus groups 😍

Currently, am developing passion for global health and equity of health, especially in low- and middle-income countries. Also, wonder if/how my kind of science does any tangible good, compared to say fine art or communities, especially during a time that wars and genocides are going on.
